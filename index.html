Through our experience and expertise in the baking industry, we are able to provide our customers with the best quality products and the best service at an affordable price.

Welcome

The Merritt Bakery is where you can find the best in gourmet cakes for any special occasion. Weddings, anniversaries, company celebrations, birthday parties, holidays and more... We bring you great taste, elegance and variety - suitable for any event.

Established in 1952, we have been part of the Lake Merritt community serving delicious comfort food every day of the week. Our philosophy is simple, make it like Mom. Breakfast, lunch and dinner served all day. We are open every day of the year.

Be sure to save room for dessert or stop by the bakery after your meal to pick up something for later. Our bakery offers a huge selection of fresh baked cakes, cookies, pies and delicious ice cream. We would like to recommend a square of our Strawberry Dream Cake.

Planning a party? Let us do the work for you. We will prepare our World Famous Fried Chicken, all the side dishes, custom create your cake, and have it ready when you are.
